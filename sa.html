Planet Earth - Quick Guides

Here are some quick guides to help you understand our planet better. Click any link to learn more.

Steps to protect our planet

  1. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
  2. Conserve Water
  3. Use Renewable Energy
  4. Plant Trees
  5. Reduce Carbon Footprint

Incredible Natural Wonders

  • Amazon Rainforest - home to 10% of the world's biodiversity
  • Great Barrier Reef - the world's largest coral reef system
  • Mount Everest - the highest mountain on Earth
  • Sahara Desert - the largest hot desert on Earth
  • Grand Canyon - a natural wonder carved by the Colorado River

Key Terms

Climate change
Long-term shifts in global or regional climate patterns.
Renewable Energy
Energy derived from natural sources that are replenished at a higher rate than they are consumed.
Biodiversity
The variety of life in a particular habitat or ecosystem.
